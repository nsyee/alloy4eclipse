Getting Additional Help

You can get more detailed information about Alloy syntax, philosophy, and case studies from the Alloy Reference Manual. Alloy itself can be downloaded from the Alloy Home page.

Please feel free to direct questions and comments (about this tutorial, about Alloy in general, or about related research) to the Alloy mailing list, XalloyX@mit.edu, or to the tutorial authors, XrseaterX@mit.edu (Rob Seater) and XgdennisX@mit.edu (Greg Dennis). Remove the "X" from the beginning and end of each email address. More clever spam protection is coming soon.
